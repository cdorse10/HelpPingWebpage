Overview

Currently the average response time is eight minutes and the average response time for a policeman is 18 minutes. Our objective is to reduce these times by implementing a more effecient system that is easier to use.

Our goals

  • Create a safe envirnmont for the user and responder
  • Enhance the ability to transmit data in real time
  • Better the relationship between organizations and responders
  • Give everyone the knowledge they can always contact a responder

How do we do this

We have designed a phone application that tracks you real time and has a emergency button that you can press if you are ever in danger. There are times where you might not have the ability to talk on the phone to a responder. It will also locate the first responders that are around your location and notify them of what is going on.
